RULES

How to play the GAME?


- You have to predict for ALL the Matches as per the schedule.
- The predictions will freeze 1 hour before the scheduled match timing for the respective match and all are expected to place the predictions before that.
- You need to predict the following for every match:
1. Winner – Name of the Team or a “Draw” for a Tie / Match Abandoned
2. Toss – Team Winning the Toss
- Be careful while adding predictions, it cannot be changed once you have saved the prediction.

How to score POINTS?


- Points for Predictions:
If you predict the Winner correctly, you get 100 points else you lose 50 points.
You can also predict a DRAW. (A draw is considered in case of a Tie and also if the match is abandoned due to weather or any other external factors!)
If you predict a draw and the match ends in a draw you get 100 points. If you predict a draw and the match has a winner, you lose 50 points.
If you do not predict you lose 100 points.

- Points for Toss:
If you predict the Winner of the Toss correctly, you get 50 points else you lose 25 points.
If you do not predict you lose 50 points.
